December 1, 2025 Project Log

Talked with family about school needs in the Philippines

Time Spent: 1 hour

What I Did: I spoke with my family about Macatal Elementary School in the Philippines and asked about the school’s current needs. They shared that the school still needs funding to complete its reading book collection, finish the floor tiles, add steel shelves, and improve the roof frame.

The school also provided photos, which helped me better understand the learning environment and the needs of the students.
